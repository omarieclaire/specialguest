FAQ

What is this?

An experimental marketplace where you can book strange and wonderful guests for your video meeting. Zoombombing from a better world.

How does it work?

Please select a guest and send a booking request. Please include the date, time, and duration of the meeting. If there are any specific problems we'll be helping with or situational information we should know, you can include this in your booking request.

What is the price?

What price can we set on a truly unique experience? How can we convert the limitless potential of each human moment into a flattening currency which somehow always seems to extract wealth from the many and siphon it to the few?

Okay, so what is the price?

The range is $50-$500.

I'd like to be a special guest

Tell us. If we add it to the list we'll send you a special gift.
